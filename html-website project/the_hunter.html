As darkness falls, I come alive. My time... has come.
I make my way into the night, feeling my senses awaken one by one.
The shadows are my friend as I slink along dimly lit streets and through dense vegetation.
My ears prick up at every noise, every flash of movement is caught by my keen eyes.
I am a hunter on the prowl, dealing swift death in an instant with tooth and claw.
I savor the hot blood of my latest kill as it drips down my chin.
Sinuously, I ascend to the roof tops and gaze at my realm from on high, alit by a thousand sparkling diamonds in the sky above.
Suddenly, I spot movement and in a flash, I leap from the roof to pursue this trespasser, this invader of my domain.
In a moment, I am upon him... teeth, claw, blood and sinew.
He flees, limping and whimpering in pain because nothing can stand against me.
As dawn's first rays creep over the horizon, I return to my home to rest.
Swaying with the drunken pride of a night asserting my dominance.
I climb the stairs, leap onto my bed... just as my servant awakes to wait upon my every need.

"Good morning Mr. Truffles! Who's a good boy?!"
